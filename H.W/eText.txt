His Highness Dr Shaikh Sultan Bin Mohammad Al Qasimi, 
Supreme Council Member and Ruler of Sharjah, 
attended the opening of the International Conference 
on Solutions for a Better Life at the University 
of Sharjah.The five-day International Conference is 
being hosted by the University of Sharjah 
in partnership with West Virginia State University of 
the United States and the World Health Organisation (WHO)..
